# Mission

Acquire enough understanding of Thai law and civic concepts to pass the หน้าที่พลเมือง exam at MWIT.

## Success criteria

- Can explain the meaning, types, and hierarchy of Thai law
- Can describe the elements of a state and the role of the constitution
- Can outline the structure of criminal liability and criminal penalties
- Can distinguish natural persons from juristic persons and describe legal capacity

## Constraints

- Exam is 2026-07-20 (tomorrow) — urgent, zero prior knowledge
- Content scope: four PDF lecture files (basic law, state/constitution, criminal law, civil/commercial law — persons)
