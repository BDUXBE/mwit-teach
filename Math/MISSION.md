## Course Description

Limits at infinity of functions, infinite sequences and series, L'Hôpital's Rule, related rates, graphing functions (increasing/decreasing, concavity, asymptotes), extreme value problems, area between curves, volumes of solids of revolution, arc length, and surface area.

---

## Learning Outcomes

| # | Outcome |
|---|---------|
| 1 | Apply infinite sequences and series to solve problems |
| 2 | Apply derivatives of functions to solve problems |
| 3 | Apply integrals of functions to solve problems |
