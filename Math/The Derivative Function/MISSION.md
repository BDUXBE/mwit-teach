# Mission — The Derivative Function

## Course
ค30107 Calculus & Applications · MWIT Grade 12 · AY 2569 Semester 1

## Student
Grade 12, MWIT. Classroom instruction complete. Needs retrieval practice and exam confidence.

## Immediate Goal (quiz July 20–24, 2026 — in progress)
Secure full marks on the upcoming quiz covering:
- **Related Rates** (3 pts) — set up and solve dy/dt, dx/dt problems
- **L'Hôpital's Rule** (3 pts) — recognize indeterminate forms, apply the rule correctly
- **Function Analysis I** (4 pts) — use f′ and f″ to find intervals of increase/decrease, local extrema, concavity

## Broader Goal
Develop fluency with the derivative as a tool: reading function behavior from derivatives, applying
rules under time pressure, and setting up word problems correctly.

## Success Criteria
- Can identify all standard indeterminate forms and apply L'Hôpital's Rule without prompting
- Can relate two rates via implicit differentiation and isolate the unknown rate
- Can produce a complete sign chart for f′ and f″ and classify all critical points
- Scores ≥ 9/10 on the quiz

## Constraints
- All lessons bilingual (English + Thai toggle), same format as Sequences and Series lessons
- Lessons short and completable in ≤ 15 minutes
- Focus: retrieval practice, not re-teaching — student has seen everything in class
